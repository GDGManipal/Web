GDG MANIPAL


            Google Developers Groups(GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google’s technology; everything from Android, Chrome, Drive and Google cloud platforms, to product API’s like Cast API, Maps API, and YouTube API.A GDG can take many forms-from just a few people getting together to watch our latest video ,to a large gatherings with demos and tech talks, to events like code sprints and hackathons.



            We encourage and invite Technology Enthusiasts /Developers/Designers/Interested Students/Professionals to be a part of this awesome community of likeminded personalities and come up with innovative and futuristic product ideas, that is set to redefine the digital culture and people’s lives.



            Google’s Women Techmakers program provides visibility, community, and resources for women in technology. From 2014 to present, Women Techmakers is launching global scalable initiatives and piloting new programs to support and empower women in industry.
